Our Story

Founded in 2005, Paws & Claws Veterinary Clinic has been serving the community for over 15 years. Our mission is to provide exceptional veterinary care with compassion and expertise.

We believe in treating every pet as if they were our own and building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust and mutual respect.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.
